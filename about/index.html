Background

I'm a cybersecurity engineer with over 5 years of experience in building secure, decentralized systems. I've worked with startups and enterprises to harden their infrastructure, audit smart contracts, and implement DevSecOps pipelines.

I hold the Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) and Certified Blockchain Security Professional (CBSP) certifications. I've also contributed to open‑source security tools and spoken at conferences like ETHDenver and OWASP meetups.

Approach

I believe in "security by design" – integrating security from the earliest stages of development. Whether it's a smart contract or a cloud infrastructure, I apply zero trust principles and continuous testing to minimize risk.

When I'm Not Coding

I'm probably bug hunting on Immunefi, contributing to CTF challenges, or exploring new privacy‑preserving technologies.

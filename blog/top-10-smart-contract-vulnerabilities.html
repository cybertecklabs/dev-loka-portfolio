Top 10 Smart Contract Vulnerabilities in 2025

Published: February 15, 2026 • 8 min read

Smart contracts are immutable, which makes security paramount. Here are the most common vulnerabilities I encounter during audits...

1. Reentrancy

Reentrancy attacks occur when an external contract calls back into the calling contract before the first execution is finished. Always use the checks‑effects‑interactions pattern and reentrancy guards.

2. Integer Overflows/Underflows

Although Solidity 0.8+ includes built‑in overflow checks, older versions or unsafe casts can still cause issues. Use SafeMath or compiler checks.
